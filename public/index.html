Is Free InstaSaver free?

Yes. The tool is built for free public Instagram media downloads. Only save content you own or have permission to use.

Can it download private Instagram posts?

No. Free InstaSaver does not bypass private profiles, login walls, or restricted content. It only works with public links that are reachable online.
